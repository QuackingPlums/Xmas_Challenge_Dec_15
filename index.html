Xmas Challenge @ The Triangle

December 20, 2015

Race format:

Nine (9!) rounds, four heats, seven (7!) pilots per heat.
2 minute races, no distance cap, points awarded for most laps in shortest time.
Winner of each heat calculated from all scores over all nine rounds.
